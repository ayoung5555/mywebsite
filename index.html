About

My first coding endeavor was a little batch file game I built in fifth grade. I self taught, through googling and experimenting, my first programming language. I loved (and still do) the ability to change a couple words- or even letters- and moments later be able to see the changes it brings to life. In 2018, another google brought me to Snapchat's website, and thus began my foray into AR. I became an OLC, was in a panel at SXSW, and now I'm off to college. Who knows where my next Google search will bring me?
